28 c> @param nbd On entry nbd represents the type of bounds imposed on the
29 c> variables, and must be specified as follows:
30 c> nbd(i)=<ul><li>0 if x(i) is unbounded,</li>

35 c>
36 c> @param f On first entry f is unspecified.<br/>
37 c> On final exit f is the value of the function at x.
38 c>

40 c> On final exit g is the value of the gradient at x.
41 c>
42 c> @param factr On entry factr >= 0 is specified by the user. The iteration
43 c> will stop when<br/>
44 c> (f^k - f^{k+1})/max{|f^k|,|f^{k+1}|,1} <= factr*epsmch<br/>
45 c> where epsmch is the machine precision, which is automatically
46 c> generated by the code.<br/>
47 c> Typical values for factr:<ul>
48 c> <li>1.d+12 for low accuracy;</li>
49 c> <li>1.d+7 for moderate accuracy;</li>
50 c> <li>1.d+1 for extremely high accuracy.</li></ul>
51 c> On exit factr is unchanged.
52 c>
53 c> @param pgtol On entry pgtol >= 0 is specified by the user. The iteration
54 c> will stop when<br/>
55 c> max{|proj g_i | i = 1, ..., n} <= pgtol<br/>
56 c> where pg_i is the ith component of the projected gradient.<br/>
57 c> On exit pgtol is unchanged.
