5 c> dtrsl solves systems of the form

8 c> or

10 c>
11 c> where t is a triangular matrix of order n. here trans(t)

13 c> @param t On entry, t contains the matrix of the system. the zero
14 c> elements of the matrix are not referenced, and
15 c> the corresponding elements of the array can be
16 c> used to store other information.
17 c> @param ldt On entry, ldt is the leading dimension of the array t.
18 c> @param n On entry, n is the order of the system.
19 c> @param b On entry, b contains the right hand side of the system.<br/>
20 c> On exit, b contains the solution, if info .eq. 0.
21 c> otherwise b is unaltered.
22 c> @param job On entry, job specifies what kind of system is to be solved.
23 c> if job is<ul>
24 c> <li>00 solve t*x=b, t lower triangular,</li>
